Frequently Asked Questions

We have created a few set of questions which you might have in your mind. If you want to know something else, Please feel free to ask anything in our contact us section.

Does this app split the bill or tip ?

You can enter the bill amount and the app will split the bill amount including the tip. There is no seperate option of splitting the tip itself.

Is there a feature of attaching a bill or clicking the photo of the bill itself in Save bill option?

Yes, Both the options are available. You can either attach a bill from your gallery or you can click image of bill then and there to store for future referrence.

How does this app validate a bill ?

We did a research on how a restaurant bill is calculated and what kind of taxes prevail in a particular country and its respective states, we have applied a logic to automatically validate the bill based on bill value & location of the generated bill.

How many countries does the guide covers and how did you get the data for that ?

Currently we have covered 12 countries but we will be updating the app soon with more countries and their tipping cultures. A careful research was done before presenting any data on the application. It was collected and compiled from a lot of trusted sources.

How many currencies does the app support ? How many languages does the app support.

Currently we support conversion in 32 currencies. The app is available in 6 languages which includes English(Default), Dutch, French, Italian, German & Hindi. We are constantly working on providing translations in more languages and improving the existing ones.
